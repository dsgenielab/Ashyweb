Struck by Lighting

Sandra's Story

This is the story of a time my best friend Sandra was struck by lighting on her way to Burger King

On a warm and muddy day in July, Sandra was walking from a volleyball tournament. Her team performed the best ever, and ended up deadlast. While this was disappointing, she was happy that she did her best and wasn't benched like she usually is. After, a long and active day, Sandra could never stop dreaming about onion rings and a whopper - her favorite consolation prize after a loss.

When she got close to the restaurant, she made a list in her mind to get:

  • Onion rings
  • Sprite
  • Whopper
